Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 6, 2025

Notes is a free and open-source Chrome extension that allows users to create and manage notes directly in their browser.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal data or usage information.

Storage of Notes

All notes and data are stored locally in your browser's storage (Chrome Storage API). None of your notes ever leave your device.

No Third-Party Access

There is no login, no Google sign-in, and no third-party services involved in the extension.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer any user information because we don't collect any in the first place.

Analytics

This extension does not use Google Analytics or any other tracking tools.

Security

Since no data leaves your browser, your notes are as secure as your local device.

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, feel free to contact us via the extension’s GitHub page or Chrome Web Store listing.
